The Readers Project, as a whole, was selected for inclusion in the Electronic Literature Organization's 2016 Electronic Literature Collection 3.

This page will soon provide downloads and links for work that is representative of Project outcomes, current as of early 2016, intended as our curated contribution to ELC3.

In the meantime – and at any time for that matter – you are welcome to explore The Readers Project site as it continues to evolve.

The following links are already available from the main site's menus. These give access to runnable jar files only. We are – as of mid-Feb 2016 – in the process of making new downloadable applications for Mac, PC, and Linux.

  • •Poetic Caption (vintage 2010-11)
  • •Misspelt Landings (vintage 2009-10)

How It Is in Common Tongues, 2012, is an artists' book produced in an edition limited to 256 copies, numbered 0-ff. The first f copies are not in circulation.
